Ingredients

  • 200g Noodles
  • 2 tsp dark soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Chinese Broccoli – trim ends, cut into 7.5cm/3" pieces. Separate leaves from stems. Cut thick stems in half vertically so they're no wider than 0.8cm / 0.3" thick.
  2. Add chicken back in: Quickly add chicken and veg back in, and toss to disperse. Serve immediately!
  3. Caramelise noodles: Return wok to stove, heat 2 tbsp oil over high heat until it starts smoking (HOT is key!). Add noodles and Sauce. Toss as few times as possible to disperse Sauce and make edges of noodles caramelise – about 1 to 1 1/2 minutes.
